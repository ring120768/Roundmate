# Pugsie PA — sorted.

**For the lads who are mustard on the tools and murder on the paperwork.**

You're brilliant at the windows. The books? Different story. Invoices you never send, money you're owed and can't remember, customers who quietly fall off the round. It all leaks away while you're busy doing the actual job.

Pugsie PA does the boring money stuff for you. Finish a clean, tick one box on your phone, and it handles the rest — sends the invoice, logs who's paid, books the next visit, fires off the "see you on the [date]" confirmation. Done before the suds dry. Pub by 3, books still straight.

## Straight up, what it does for you

- **Gets you paid.** Every job invoiced the second you tick it off — no more "did I bill that one?" And one screen tells you exactly who owes you and how much. (Go on — how much are you owed right now? Thought so.)
- **No more evening paperwork.** The admin you've been dodging happens in one tap, from the van, while you're still in your boots.
- **Keeps your round full.** Books the next visit automatically and confirms it with the customer, so nobody drifts off and your repeat work stays nailed down.
- **Less driving, more cleaning.** Shows you which customers are near each other, so you knock out a whole postcode in one go instead of tearing across town burning diesel.
- **Makes you look proper.** Tidy, branded invoices and confirmations land in their inbox — looks like a real firm, gets you paid quicker, and heads off the "did you even turn up?" arguments.
- **It's just on your phone.** Big buttons, no laptop, no spreadsheets, no faff. Built for blokes up a ladder, not accountants at a desk.

## The bottom line

It'll dig out money you've forgotten you're even owed — probably pays for itself before teatime. For about the price of two cleans a month, your admin does itself and you get your evenings back.

**Do the windows. Tick the box. Get to the pub. The money sorts itself.**
